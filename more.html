Places to visit in Mexico City

1 Museo de Frida Kahlo Coyoacán, CDMX Frida Kahlo was a famous Mexican artist. Her beatiful home is a muesum for the public to see.
2 Castillo de Chapultepec Bosque de Chapultepec, CDMX A historic castle that started construction in 1785 as a residence but now headquarters of the National Muesum of History. Which keeps the memory of history of Mexico from the conquest of Tenochtitlan of the Mexican Revolution.
3 Catedral Metropolitana de la Ciudad de México Centro Histórico, CDMX Many people practice religion in Mexico and the main religion practice is Catholicism. These cathedral is located in the Zocalo next to Palacio Nacional and Templo Mayo de Mexico.
4 Museo del Templo Mayor Centro Histórico, CDMX This is a muesum that is dedicated to the Aztecs, where ruins were discovered in the middle of the city.
